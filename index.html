About Us

We are a game studio located in southern part of Bangladesh. We started our journey in early 2020. We are passionate at making games, we believe that a good games can make a gamer happy. So we try to make our games good and fun. We make games mainly for mobile platform. But we port them to PC also.

We like stories. Game is a great medium to tell a story to the players. Hence we make our games with story. Our games contain stories about society, life, politics and so on.

We have just started our journey and there is a long way to go. We deeply hope and believe that we can continue this and bring more joy to gamers.

Our Goal

Nowadays most of the mobile games are filled with ads that pop out of nowhere whennplaying. This completely ruins gaming experience. Also, to complete these games, you often need to pay hundreds of bucks.

Our goal is to make games without these things. Ad should not appear while playing and purchases should be optional. Gamers should not be forced on unnecessary payment
